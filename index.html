Hello World

I’m Farren’s domain,
How do you do~
There’s not much here,
But isn’t that cool too?

Her portfolio is elsewhere
So here lies in lieu
some words, arranged carefully.
Me!—her precious WWW.

There are no ads here
And no products sold either
No donations solicited
Just thoughts and whitespace breather

So take your time here
And let the emptiness stew.
Try not to think of other sites
Like ones I don’t live up to.
‘Cause here, little ol’ me will have to do.
